Business Insights

  • Target customers are those with higher income and higher spending score; these customers are best for promotions and premium services.
  • Low spend, high income customers may need engagement campaigns or festival discounts.
  • Young low-income, high-spending customers can be targeted with value deals and loyalty programs.

Although the dataset does not contain visit frequency or festival attendance, the clustering result helps the marketing team allocate investment across customer groups and design discount campaigns.
